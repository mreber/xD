Max Reber

My name is Max Reber, Welcome to my Webpage! I practice Video, and 3D Image/Animation under the Experimental New Media umbrella. Digital practices with ceramics is something I do as well, but I'm still very new to the medium.

I create glitched, hyper-edited, abbrasive video and image work. It's fun for me to edit photos and feedback loop them into the program and layer more effects ontop the image. DEEP FRY THAT HOE!

A lot of my work is centered around social media cultures. I spend a lot of time on social media websites so I try to turn that consumption of media into words or behaviours that exist inside my videos. I wouldn't say my work is a synthesis of Internet culture fully, but of Social Media culture. I think social media almost takes a different form than the interent, it exists as it's own planet seperated from what the internet actually is. I like to think about how much of our experience online is mediated by software companies and how much of our consumption of online-media is made easier through their interfaces.
